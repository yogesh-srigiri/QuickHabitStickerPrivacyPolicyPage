Quick Habit Sticker - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Overview

Quick Habit Sticker ("Application") is a lightweight, offline-first habit tracker. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Application processes, where it is stored, and how you can contact us about privacy-related requests. This policy is designed to meet Google Play Store requirements for transparency on data collection and handling.

1. Data Collection

The Application operates primarily offline and, by default, does not collect or transmit personal data to remote servers.

  • No user accounts or login information
  • No emails or contact information collected by the app
  • No device identifiers intentionally transmitted
  • No IP addresses logged by the app
  • No analytics or usage data sent to third parties

2. Local Data Storage

All habit and progress data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms. This data remains on your device unless removed by you. Device-level backup services may include this data depending on your system settings.

3. Permissions and Notifications

  • Notifications: Used only for local habit reminders if you enable them.
  • Internet: Included for technical compatibility; the app does not transmit personal data.

4. Third-Party Libraries

The Application uses open‑source libraries such as React Native, Expo, and related community packages. These libraries operate within the app and are not used to collect or transmit personal user data.

5. Analytics & Crash Reporting

The Application does not include analytics, tracking, or crash‑reporting services that collect personal data.

6. Data Sharing & Sale

We do not share, sell, or rent personal information to third parties.

7. Security

Data is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping your device secure with system updates and screen‑lock protection.

8. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect personal information from children and is safe for general audiences.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the Application from your device.

10. Legal Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under privacy laws such as GDPR or CCPA. Because the app stores data only locally and does not collect personal data centrally, requests can typically be fulfilled by clearing local app data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date above.
